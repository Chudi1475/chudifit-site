No influencer workouts. Triceps before biceps so long arms actually fill out. Incline work instead of living on flat bench. Leg day from day one so you're never top-heavy on pencil legs. Stick to the basics and your dream physique will be dialing your phone.

02 / Mobility

The four things that decide whether you lift pain-free for years or end up under an MRI machine. A real stretching routine for upper and lower body, built in from week one, so you can hit deep range of motion on a tall frame safely.

03 / Nutrition & recovery

Fueled for a bigger frame.

01 / The wake-up call

In 2020 my friends invited me to a backyard workout. The whole thing was 100 pushups. They breezed through theirs, dancing and filming videos. I stalled at 50 with dead arms. That night, still cramping, I told myself I had to make something happen.

02 / The grind

Dumbbells were sold out everywhere during COVID, so I stood in line at opening just to buy a pair of 15s. From that day it was 2am workouts, seven days a week. Just me, two dumbbells, and YouTube. I told nobody. Months later a friend saw me and went "bro, you look built." Someone else noticed before I let myself believe it. That was the day I knew.

03 / Why I coach

Six years and a few hard-earned injuries later, I coach tall athletes so you get the shortcut I never had. If you can build a body you never thought was possible, you can build anything. The life you and your mom been praying for is right around the corner. All it takes is consistency and a little bit of obsession.

FAQ

Questions tall athletes ask.

Do I really need to be 6'2"+?

The system is built specifically for tall frames. If you're around or above 6'2", this is for you. Not sure you fit? Book a call and we'll talk it through honestly.

I've got nagging injuries. Can I still train?

I've trained through a cooked rotator cuff, aching knees and a bad ankle, and mobility work is a pillar of this program for exactly that reason. We build around what's hurt, strengthen what caused it, and keep you out from under the MRI machine. Serious medical stuff goes to a doctor first, always.

What exactly do I get?

A fully custom training program built for your height, a nutrition and recovery plan, mobility and joint work, weekly check-ins, and direct 1-on-1 access to me through the whole 180 days.

I'm a beginner. Is this for me?

I started with two 15-pound dumbbells and couldn't finish 100 pushups. Whether you're brand new or stuck after years of training, the plan starts from where you are right now.

How much does coaching cost?

This is a premium 1-on-1 program, not a cheap template. We cover the investment on the strategy call once we both know it's the right fit. The call itself is free and no pressure.

What happens on the strategy call?

It's a 30-minute Zoom call. We break down your current training and nutrition, pinpoint what's holding you back, and decide together whether 1-on-1 coaching is your right next step.
